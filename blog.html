Smile AI: Giving Dentists an Intelligent Assistant

Smile AI, created by Mathesis Labs, is transforming dentistry by giving dentists an intelligent assistant that handles everything from diagnostics to treatment planning, allowing practitioners to focus more on patient care and less on administrative tasks.

Modern dental practices face numerous challenges: increasing patient loads, complex treatment planning, maintaining accuracy in diagnostics, and keeping up with the latest research and techniques. Smile AI was designed to address all these pain points through advanced artificial intelligence.

Our platform serves as a comprehensive AI assistant for dentists. It analyzes X-rays and scans with diagnostic accuracy that rivals and often exceeds human experts.
